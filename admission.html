Admission Information

Admission Criteria

  •   3 Years Diploma: Passed 10th, General Category 50% and 45% for reserve category or PAT-2016.
  •   2nd Year Lateral Entry: 12th with Math, Physics, Chemistry or ITI Two Years,

PAT Candidate (All Category)

  •  Candidate have must pass 10th standard with at least 35% marks from a recognized Board/ University.

Without PAT Candidate

  •   Candidate belongs to General category have must pass 10 th standard with at least 50% marks from a recognized Board/ University.
  •   Candidate belongs to SC, ST and OBC category have must pass 10 th standard with at least 45% marks from a recognized Board/ University.

LEET Candidate (All Category)

  •   Candidate have must pass 12 th Science (with mathematics as one of the subject) or 12 th Science with vocational or Science with technical or 10 + (2 years ITI/COE) from recognized Board/ University.

Without LEET Candidate

  •   Candidate Belongs to General category have must pass 12 th Science (with mathematics as one of the subject) or 12 th Science with vocational or Science with technical or 10 th + (2 years ITI/COE) from recognized Board/ University with at least 50% Marks.
  •   Candidate belongs to Reserve category have must pass 12 th Science (with mathematics as one of the subject) or 12 th Science with vocational or Science with technical or 10 + (2 years ITI/COE) from recognized Board/ University with at least 45% marks.
