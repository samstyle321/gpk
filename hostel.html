HOSTEL

Boys Hostel

Hostel facility for boys is available in the campus. The hostel environment is conducive to learning and tension free to enable the students to imbibe the spirit of healthy comradeship.

The Hostel is facilated with capacity of 200 students.In the hostel, each room is facilited with three beds, almiras with study table & chair also. The college provides hot water heater facility to all the hostellers. Each of the hostels has a mess to ensure that all the resident students get wholesome, nutritious and hygienically well cooked meals.


Other Facilities

  •  Hostelers are allow to play in the playground and Multi-purpose Hall till 7:30 p.m

  •  Tewnty four hour running water and electricity facility.

  •  Hostel is fully under the survilence of camera.

  •  The mess has good food facility with fixed price and menu to hostelers.
